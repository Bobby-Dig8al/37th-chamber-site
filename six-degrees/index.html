6 Degrees of Giants

Help us reach the shoulders of our giants.

If I have seen further, it’s by standing on the shoulders of giants. Six degrees of separation says everyone is reachable through a handful of warm hands. So let’s find out — together. Pick a giant, start a chain, and pass it to someone you actually know. Each tag is one jump closer. The view is not ours alone.

The first rule
Read this before you play

Relay, not swarm.

We are not trying to swarm them — we could do that ourselves. A flood of DMs is trivial; anyone can brute-force an inbox. The entire dignity and value of this is the single gracious chain. Volume is the opposite of the point.

One chain, not a mob. We honor the path; we don’t hunt the person.

The win is one warm hand reaching the next, all the way to the giant — framed as “we’d love to share something with you,” never “we’re hunting you.” That constraint is what makes it meaningful, and what makes the giants want to be reached this way. Protect the giants and it stays beautiful.

One chain A single relay per team. Each person passes it onward to one real connection — not the whole crowd piling on at once.
A real hand You only tag someone you actually know. The public tag is the verifiable jump — a real relationship, not a cold blast.
A warm intro The goal is a gracious introduction that honors the giant — loving-grace, never Big-Brother. We share; we don’t spam.
How to play

Pick a team. Start a chain. Pass it on.

1 Pick a team. Choose a giant from the roster below — the one you feel closest to, or the one you think you can actually help reach.
2 Start a chain. Post publicly that you’re on Team [Giant], and tag the next person you actually know who’s a step closer to them.
3 Each tag is a jump. They tag the next real hand. The relay continues — one gracious link at a time — until the chain reaches the giant.
Pick your team → v0 — the chain runs on public tags out in the open, no account needed. The live tracker & leaderboard arrive with the full chamber.
