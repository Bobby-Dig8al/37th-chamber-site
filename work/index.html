What I do

AI-integration consulting

Pick the right AI tools for the job, wire them into your existing workflow, and put the guardrails around them so the team uses them well.

  • Audit your current AI stack — what’s working, what’s leaking, what to replace
  • Workflow design — where humans stay in the loop, where AI accelerates
  • Compliance-aware from the start — the right tool for a regulated workflow is not always the popular one

Forensic AI-output audit

Take a body of AI-generated work — reports, code, emails, briefs — and grade it against the truth. Catch the hallucinations before they reach a client, a regulator, or a stakeholder.

  • Claim-by-claim verification against primary sources
  • Citation integrity check — does the cited paper say what the AI says it says?
  • Plain-language audit report you can act on

Custom AI tooling for your craft

If the off-the-shelf tools don’t fit, we build the one that does — small, sharp, owned by you. Same principle as the chambers: understand everything; outsource nothing blindly.

  • Internal assistants tuned to your domain (medical, legal, research, creative)
  • Document-pipeline tooling — ingest, search, summarize, verify
  • Lightweight, deployable, and auditable — the model is a black box; your use of it isn’t
