Friday — Shattered Glass. A 12-fold iris of bold, fractured glass planes: fewer and larger shards, each a faceted pane with a bright specular edge and a refraction-split fringe of color along its rim. High-contrast, dramatic, near-black between the panes — light caught in broken crystal. For his mother, who loved kaleidoscopes.
